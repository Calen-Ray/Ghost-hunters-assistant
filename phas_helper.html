  • Spirit

    Spirits are very common ghosts. They are very powerful, but passive, only attacking when they need to.

    They defend their place of death to the utmost degree, killing anyone that is caught overstaying their welcome.

    • EMF 5
    • Spirit Box
    • Ghost Writing

    Strength: Spirits have no outstanding strengths.

    Weakness: A Spirit can be temporarily stopped by burning Smudge Sticks near them.

    Spirits have no unique behavior and can easily be mistaken for another entity. Once the sanity average is below 50%, smudge the ghost while it is not hunting. If it hunts within the next three minutes, it is not a Spirit.

    Note: Due to the Spirit's generic behavior, it's easily mistaken for other ghost types.

    Nightmare Tip: Players should wait for a hunt or a burnt crucifix to confirm that the average sanity is low enough.

  • Poltergeist

    One of the most famous ghosts, the Poltergeist. Known to manipulate objects around it to spread fear into its victims.

    • Spirit Box
    • Fingerprints
    • Ghost Writing

    Strength: Poltergeist can throw multiple objects at once, and with great force.

    Weakness: With nothing to throw, Poltergeists become powerless.

    To test if it is a Poltergeist, one can scatter items in small piles around the ghost room. If an object is flung with force, or multiple objects are thrown, it is likely a Poltergeist.

    Note: There is some overlap of how frequently other ghost types can throw items, this trait may be harder to discern in small rooms.

    Nightmare Tip: No extra information at this time.

  • Mare

    A Mare is the source of all nightmares, making it most powerful in the dark.

    • Spirit Box
    • Ghost Orb
    • Ghost Writing

    Strength: A Mare will have an increased chance to attack in the dark.

    Weakness: Turning the lights on around the Mare will lower its chance to attack.

    Mares cannot turn lights on, and are more likely to turn the lights off as an interaction, or perform light-shattering events. If a light, TV, or computer is activated within 4 metres of the Mare, it might promptly switch off. This has a minimum 10-second cooldown between interactions. Finally, when a Mare roams, it is more likely to choose an unlit room.

    Note: If the lights are off, the hunt sanity threshold is 60%, if the lights are on, it is decreased to 40%. Other sources of light, such as brought-in equipment and candles, do not affect this threshold.

    Nightmare Tip: If the lights near the ghost are consistently on, and it frequently changes its favorite room to a dark area, it could be a Mare.

  • Demon

    A Demon is one of the worst ghosts you can encounter. It has been known to attack without reason.

    • Fingerprints
    • Ghost Writing
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: Demons will initiate hunts more often than other ghosts.

    Weakness: Demons fear the Crucifix and will be less aggressive near one.

    To identify a demon, monitor hunt cooldown times and early hunt signs. Conserve sprint for quick escapes during sudden hunts due to their aggressiveness. Crucifixes have an extended range for easier hunt prevention. Demons can mislead with interactions to hide their true location, so diligently verify the correct Ghost Room using tools like the Thermometer.

    Note: A Demon has a normal hunt sanity threshold of 70%, along with this they have a cooldown of 20 seconds between hunts. Smudging a demon will prevent a hunt for 60 seconds. As a weakness, the effective range of a crucifix is 5 metres instead of the standard 3 metres.

    Nightmare Tip: None Required.

  • Yokai

    Yokai are common ghosts that are attracted to human voices. They can usually be found haunting family homes.

    • Spirit Box
    • Ghost Orb
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Talking near a Yokai will anger it, increasing the chance of an attack.

    Weakness: When hunting, a Yokai can only hear voices close to it.

  • Myling

    A Myling is a very vocal and active ghost. They are rumoured to be quiet when hunting their prey.

    • EMF 5
    • Fingerprints
    • Ghost Writing

    Strength: A Myling is known to be quieter when hunting.

    Weakness: Mylings make more frequent paranormal sounds.

  • Raiju

    A Raiju is a demon that thrives on electrical current.

    While generally calm, they can become agitated when overwhelmed with power.

    • EMF 5
    • Ghost Orb
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: A Raiju can siphon power from nearby electrical devices, making it move faster.

    Weakness: Raiju are constantly disrupting electronic equipment when attacking, making it easier to track.

  • Moroi

    Moroi have risen from the grave to drain energy from the living.

    • Spirit Box
    • Ghost Writing
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: The weaker their victims, the stronger the Moroi becomes.

    Weakness: Moroi suffer from hyperosmia, weakening them for longer periods.

  • Wraith

    Wraiths are one of the most dangerous ghosts you will find.

    It is also the only known ghost that has the ability of flight and has sometimes been known to travel through walls.

    • EMF 5
    • Spirit Box
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Wraiths almost never touch the ground, meaning it can't be tracked by footsteps.

    Weakness: Wraiths are afraid of Salt and will actively avoid it.

  • Banshee

    The singing siren, known for attracting its victims through song.

    It has been known to single out its prey before making a killing blow.

    • Fingerprints
    • Ghost Orb
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Banshees will weaken their target before striking.

    Weakness: Banshees can sometimes be heard screaming with a parabolic microphone.

  • Revenant

    A Revenant is a violent ghost that will attack indiscriminately.

    Their speed can be deceiving,
    as they are slow while dormant; however, as soon as they hunt they can move incredibly fast.
    • Ghost Orb
    • Ghost Writing
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: A Revenant will travel at a significantly faster speed when hunting their prey.

    Weakness: Hiding from the Revenant will cause it to move very slowly.

  • Yurei

    A Yurei is a ghost that has returned to the physical world, usually for the purpose of revenge or hatred.

    • Ghost Orb
    • Freezing Temps
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Yureis have been known to have a stronger effect on people sanity.

    Weakness: Smudging the Yurei's place of death will trap it temporarily, reducing how much it wanders.

  • Hantu

    A Hantu is a rare ghost that thrives in the coldest climates. The cold seems to make them more aggressive and empowered.

    • Fingerprints
    • Ghost Orb
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: Lower temperatures allow the Hantu to move at faster speeds.

    Weakness: Hantus move slower in warmer areas.

  • Onryo

    The Onryo is often referred to as "The Wrathful Spirit". It steals souls from dying victims' bodies to seek revenge.

    This ghost has been known to fear any form of fire, and will do anything to be far from it.

    • Spirit Box
    • Ghost Orb
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: Extinguishing a flame can cause an Onryo to attack.

    Weakness: When threatened, this ghost will be less likely to hunt.

  • Obake

    Obake are terrifying shape-shifters, capable of taking on many forms.

    They have been seen taking on humanoid shapes to attract their prey.

    • EMF 5
    • Fingerprints
    • Ghost Orb

    Strength: When interacting with the environment, an Obake will rarely leave a trace.

    Weakness: Sometimes this ghost will shapeshift, leaving behind unique evidence.

  • Deogen

    Sometimes surrounded by an endless fog, Deogen have been eluding ghost hunters for years.

    These ghosts have been reported to find even the most hidden prey, before stalking them into exhaustion.

    • Spirit Box
    • Ghost Writing
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Deogens constantly sense the living. You can run but you can't hide.

    Weakness: Deogens require a lot of energy to form and will move very slowly when approaching its victim.

  • Phantom

    A Phantom is a ghost that can possess the living, inducing fear into those around it.

    They are most commonly summoned from Ouija Boards.

    • Spirit Box
    • Fingerprints
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Looking at a Phantom will drop your sanity considerably faster.

    Weakness: Taking a photo of the Phantom will make it temporarily disappear.

  • Jinn

    A Jinn is a territorial ghost that will attack when threatened.

    It has also been known to be able to travel at significant speed.

    • EMF 5
    • Fingerprints
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: A Jinn will travel at a faster speed if its victim is far away.

    Weakness: Turning off the location's power source will prevent the Jinn from using its ability.

  • Shade

    A Shade is known to be very shy.

    There is evidence to suggest that a Shade will stop all paranormal activity if there are people nearby.

    • EMF 5
    • Ghost Writing
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: Shades are much harder to find.

    Weakness: The ghost will not enter a hunt if there are people nearby.

  • Oni

    Onis love to scare their victims as much as possible before attacking.

    They are often seen in their physical form, guarding their place of death.

    • EMF 5
    • Freezing Temps
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Oni are more active whilst people are nearby and will drain their sanity faster when manifesting.

    Weakness: Oni disappear less often while hunting their prey.

  • Goryo

    When a Goryo passes through a DOTS projector, using a video camera is the only way to see it.

    • EMF 5
    • Fingerprints
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: A Goryo will usually only show itself on camera if there are no people nearby.

    Weakness: They are rarely seen far from their place of death.

  • The Twins

    These ghosts have been reported to mimic each other's actions. They alternate their attacks to confuse their prey.

    • EMF 5
    • Spirit Box
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: Either Twin can be angered and initiate an attack on their prey.

    Weakness: The Twins will often interact with the environment at the same time.

  • The Mimic

    The Mimic is an elusive, mysterious, copycat ghost that mirrors traits and behaviours from others, including other ghost types.

    • Spirit Box
    • Fingerprints
    • Freezing Temps

    Strength: We're unsure what this ghost is capable of. Be careful.

    Weakness: Several reports have noted ghost orb sightings near mimics.

  • Thaye

    Thaye have been known to rapidly age over time, even in the afterlife.

    From what we've learned, they seem to deteriorate faster while within the presence of the living.

    • Ghost Orb
    • Ghost Writing
    • D.O.T.S Projector

    Strength: Upon entering the location, Thaye will become active, defensive and agile

    Weakness: Thaye will weaken over time, making them weaker, slower and less aggressive
